# Food and Farming Loop

## Purpose

Make food production depend on environmental engineering, not only crop placement.

## Farming Constraints

- Crop growth depends on:
  - temperature band
  - atmosphere composition
  - irrigation fluid
  - lighting policy

## Food Pipeline

1. Raw crop production.
2. Processing/cooking at stations.
3. Storage with spoilage rules.
4. Consumption with quality modifiers.

## Spoilage

- Food has shelf life that degrades faster in hot/polluted rooms.
- Refrigerated storage extends life with power and heat tradeoff.

## Quality Tiers

- `Poor`: basic calories, neutral or slight stress penalty.
- `Standard`: stable morale.
- `High`: stress reduction and better stamina recovery.

## Failure Pressure

- Poor environmental control lowers yield and causes supply volatility.
