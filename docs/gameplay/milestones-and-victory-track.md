# Milestones and Victory Track

## Purpose

Provide clear mid/late-game direction beyond open-ended base building.

## Milestone Sequence

1. `Air Stable`: breathable habitat sustained for target duration.
2. `Water Stable`: non-depleting water loop with recycling.
3. `Power Stable`: uninterrupted critical power uptime target.
4. `Thermal Stable`: no overheat shutdowns for target duration.
5. `Closed Loop Colony`: oxygen, water, food, and power all stable.
6. `Space Ready`: sustained operations in space-exposed zone.

## Rewards

- Unlocks advanced blueprints and efficiency modifiers.
- Milestone badges/logbook entries.

## Victory States

- Soft victory: achieve closed-loop colony milestone.
- Extended victory: complete space-ready objective chain.
